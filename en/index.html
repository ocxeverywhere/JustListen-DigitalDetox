Android App

JustListen: Youtube as Podcast turns YouTube content into listening-friendly sessions.

When you have more videos than time, JustListen: Youtube as Podcast uses a YouTube link to retrieve captions, optionally translate them, apply rule-based cleanup, and generate an audio session with Cloud Text-to-Speech. You can also send text or a YouTube link to the app from the Android share menu to start a session.

Start with a link or Android sharing

Enter a video URL, or send text or a link from the Android share menu, to retrieve captions and source details for a session.

Optional translation and rule-based cleanup

Optionally translate captions and use rules to reduce repetition, extra whitespace, and other text that is difficult to hear.

Cloud TTS, local library, and background playback

Cloud Text-to-Speech creates the audio; saved sessions stay in your device library and can keep playing when the app is closed or the screen is locked.
