Android App

JustListen turns YouTube content into listening-friendly sessions.

When you have more videos than time, JustListen uses a YouTube link to retrieve captions, organize the content, and generate an audio session you can follow on the go. It is built for digital detox, learning, and catching up on useful video content without always needing the screen.

Create sessions from YouTube links

Enter a video URL to retrieve captions and source details, then turn them into a ready-to-listen session.

AI-based organization and summaries

Refine long captions into easier-to-read sentences and summarize key points so the flow is easier to grasp.

Background playback and replay

Saved sessions stay in your library and generated audio can keep playing even when the app is closed or the screen is locked.
